Biography

Sharline Gruenzner

I was born on April 25, 1997, in Clarksville, Tn. I am a Photography Major at Memphis College of Art. When starting out in the art world I wasn't initially a photographer. I started out drawing and then did some painting but wasn't something that I enjoyed and had fun with. Even though the only camera I had was from a phone I jumped into that world without realizing it at the time, I took photos all the time.


When applying for colleges I didn't know what I wanted to do so my portfolio consisted of my drawings and paintings because thoughts what I thought I liked to do. However, once I did my first year of college doing the basic required classes which included drawing classes I realized that I wasn't in love with that side of art so I explored different areas and I kept coming back to Photography. Even though I do get stressed and frustrated with it sometimes, I still enjoy what I'm doing and in the end it is something that I love. At this point in my life photography only stresses me out because I'm in college and most things are on a time crunch, and I know that it won't be like that forever and that is what I hold on to.
